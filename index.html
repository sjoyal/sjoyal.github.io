About

Hello. I'm Sean -- an aspiring front end developer from Jacksonville, FL. This is a website. If you're here, either A) I know you, or B) you're stalking me on social media. Either way, I won't judge you. The purpose of this site is to help me establish my online 'brand', and to reveal a little about who I am. If you're interested in those things, read my blogs.


As background, I'm from Jacksonville, FL, and I'm kind of proud of it. I attended a couple of colleges and got a couple degrees. But, after 10 years working in various marketing and communications roles, I ultimately decided to pursue something completely different. I now get excited and dream about JavaScript, among everything else I'm trying to learn as a front-end developer. I'm still pretty green and have a lot to learn, but the prospect of continuous learning and the chance to create fuels my passion for development.


If you're interested in my work, I've included some of my projects in the portfolio section, and you can download my resume. Also, it ceases to be stalking if we actually connect on social media. Reach out. Links are below.
