about

Gator alum and a tech newb. I'd rather be traveling.

Hello. My name is Sean. I'm an aspiring front end developer from Jacksonville, FL. This is a website. If you're here, either A) I know you, or B) you're stalking me on social media. Either way, I won't judge you. The purpose of this site is to help me establish my online 'brand', and to reveal a little about who I am. More simply, it should help me get a job with a team that is a cultural fit, maybe? If that's what you're here for, read my blogs. Hopefully I've fixed the site, and they're working by now.

As background, I'm from Jacksonville, FL and proud of it. I went to the University of Florida and graduated with a BSBA in marketing in 2005. 10 years and a graduate degree later, I've worked in various marketing and communications roles, but have ultimately decided to pursue something entirely different. I've recently jumped head first into the tech world. I now get excited and dream about JavaScript, among other things, though I'm still very green and have a lot to learn. I'm accepting applications for a mentor if you or someone you know is interested. Contact me via social media if you'd like to connect. We both know you already have the links.
